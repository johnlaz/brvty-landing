Welcome to BRVTY
Book Intelligence for your commute.

BRVTY turns any book into a structured summary and high-fidelity audio narration — in seconds. Generate a thesis, key pillars, action items, and listen to it on the way to work.
Everything stays on your device. No account required.
🔑
Get Your Free Key
BRVTY uses Google's Gemini AI. You'll need a free API key to generate summaries and audio.
Step 1 — Visit Google AI Studio Go to aistudio.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
Step 2 — Create a key Click Get API Key → Create API key. Copy it.
Step 3 — Paste it in Settings Tap ⚙ in the top right → paste your key → Save. Free tier: 250 summaries/day.
✦
How It Works
Generate Type any book title (and optionally the author or paste your highlights). Tap Generate Summary. Takes about 10 seconds.
Summary Read the thesis, key pillars, deep insights, action items, and memorable quotes. Tap a Read Next book to generate that one too.
Audio Go to the Audio tab. Choose Quick (3 min), Chapters (section-by-section), or Deep Dive (15–20 min full narration). Tap Generate.
Car Mode Tap 🚗 for a fullscreen player. Your steering wheel controls work via Bluetooth.
🔤
Make It Comfortable
BRVTY lets you tune the reading experience to your preference. Find these in ⚙ Settings → Reading Experience.
Text Size — 3 options A− Compact  ·  A Default  ·  A+ Large
Default is balanced for most screens. Bump up if summaries feel dense.
Reading Font — 3 choices Cormorant — elegant editorial
Inter — clean, high readability
Georgia — warm, familiar serif
You can also switch between dark and light themes in the same section.
🎧
More Features
Audiobooks Import your own MP3, M4A, or WAV files. Stored locally, plays through the same player with bookmarks, speed controls, and car mode.
Library Vault Settings → Export Vault backs up your entire library — summaries, audio, and notes — as one file. Import it on any device.
Document Mode On the Generate tab, switch to Document mode to paste an article or report and get a full summary or direct audio read-back.
Tap ⚙ Settings → Show Intro to revisit this guide anytime.
